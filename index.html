The Story

The Shed is a product of continuous improvement. First built in 2011 - The structure transformed from organized lumber into a livable lodge. Apart from the structure materials (Lumber, and hardware) everything inside The Lodge has been recycled for new and unique uses. This backyard destination is perfect for BBQs or a Winter Workshop. This space is perfect for hosting friends for a bonfire or enjoying a cocktail party in all seasons. Enjoy the views of the pond, creek and organic garden from both of the attatched decks.
The Shed offers:

  • Fresh Spring Water
  • WiFi
  • 2 Tvs with Chromecast
  • Xbox
  • 7.1 Surround Sound
  • Desktop Computer
  • Amazon ALexa
  • CCTV security
